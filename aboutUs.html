Project Kessel is made up of a group of friends who have come together to try to raise awareness of the problems posed by space debris.

Our idea is to educate by playing to be able to publicize these problems in a more enjoyable way and without stopping learning.

One of our main objectives is also to try to get children interested in space and how to solve problems, in order to have a new generation of engineers who believe that everything is possible from a young age.
